Podcasts

The Urology Coding and Reimbursement Podcast is a weekly series produced by PRS Network specifically for urologists and their practice teams—administrators, advanced practice providers (APPs), billers, and coders. Its mission is to equip listeners with actionable coding and billing strategies that drive economic efficiency, allowing practices to focus more on patient care and less on paperwork.

Episodes typically open with coding or policy news, proceed to a deep dive on a central topic (e.g., telehealth updates, modifier nuances, CMS rules), and often conclude with audience submitted Q&A.

Episode 240, for example, features an in-depth discussion of the current overall medical business environment, from private equity to staff retention to legislative initiatives.
